Japanese cuisine

Japanese cuisine is very ornamental, pay attention to calm, elegant, peaceful, the selected materials are mainly fresh seafood and seasonal fresh vegetables, with light taste, fine processing, bright color, less greasy and other characteristics.
Natural and original flavor is the "killer mace" of Japanese cuisine. Its cooking method is delicate and exquisite, focusing on taste, touch, sight, smell and the collocation of utensils and dining environment. You can appreciate the unique "beauty", "elegance" and "quietness" of each of its plates, bowls and plates.
